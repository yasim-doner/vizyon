# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- Closed-group members tracking their personal and collective commitments (10 core missions).
- Group administrators who manage rosters, view logs, and maintain the central financial account (Vizyon Account).
- Users access the dashboard daily or weekly on both desktop and mobile devices to check off tasks, view potential penalties, and track group leadership stats.

## Product Purpose
- To provide a clear, daily habit-tracking dashboard that holds members accountable.
- Success looks like clear visualization of completed, in-progress, and failed tasks with no ambiguity, alongside real-time calculations of financial balances and penalties.

## Brand Personality
- Minimalist, clean, soft editorial light-mode layout (though currently operating in a dark layout, transitioning towards a clean, soft, high-contrast typography system).
- Transparent, encouraging, and structured.
- Three-word personality: Structured, Clean, Editorial.

## Anti-references
- Avoid medal, cup, or trophy icons for gamification (numerical rankings only).
- Avoid generic flat grays or low-contrast text (contrast ratios must remain high and accessible).
- Avoid SaaS-cliché layout metrics (e.g., big shiny numbers with tiny low-contrast labels and unnecessary gradient overlays).
- Avoid nested cards or side-stripe border cards.

## Design Principles
1. **Unambiguous Progress Tracking**: Highlight completed, ongoing, and failed tasks using distinct state styling rather than generic placeholders.
2. **Context-Preserving Financials**: Distinguish clearly between finalized, historical cumulative penalties (locked in at month-end) and current month forecasts (potential worst-case).
3. **Typography First**: Leverage high-contrast type scales and editorial margins instead of relying on heavy boxes, borders, and complex grids.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High text-to-background contrast ratio (>= 4.5:1).
- Accessible 3-state task colors that are distinguishable and readable.
- Mobile responsiveness and easy touch targets for small devices.
